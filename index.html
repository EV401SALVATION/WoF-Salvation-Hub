Welcome to the worlds of Wings of Fire: Salvation!

Wings of Fire: Salvation is a large-scale fanfiction project based on the Wings of Fire series that I have been working on for quite some time. It focuses on a rag-tag group of characters and their many adventures across the Wings of Fire multiverse.

On this site you'll be able to find:

  1. Links to each of the different volumes of the story.
  2. Information about the characters and worlds.
  3. Tie-in games based on the story.
  4. Papercraft based on the story.

This is an updated version of the original Wings of Fire: Salvation Hub that was created with Google Sites.
